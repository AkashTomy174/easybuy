Return Policy Details

  • Eligibility: Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all accessories included.
  • Time Frame: Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Items should be in the same condition as received, with tags attached where applicable.
  • Exclusions: Certain items like personalized products, digital downloads, and perishable goods are not eligible for return.
  • Process: Contact our support team or use the return request form in your order details.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return items bought on sale or with a discount? expand_more

Yes, sale items can be returned within our standard 30-day return window, provided they meet all other return conditions.

What if I received a damaged or defective item? expand_more

For damaged or defective items, please contact us immediately. We may arrange for a replacement or full refund without requiring you to return the item first.

How long does shipping take for returns? expand_more

Return shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your return is approved and the shipping label is generated.

Can I exchange an item instead of getting a refund? expand_more

Yes, exchanges are available for the same item in a different size/color or a different item of equal or lesser value. Contact our support team to arrange an exchange.

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer support team is here to assist you with any questions about returns or refunds.
