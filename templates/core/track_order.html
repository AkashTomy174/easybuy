Tracking FAQs

When will I receive tracking information? expand_more

You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships, typically within 1-2 business days of order confirmation.

Why hasn't my order status updated? expand_more

Order statuses update automatically. If you don't see an update within 24 hours, please check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I change my delivery address? expand_more

Address changes can only be made before the order ships. Contact support immediately if you need to update your delivery address.
